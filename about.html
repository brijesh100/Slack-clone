About Channels

Channels bring order and clarity to work – you can create them for every project, topic or team. When there’s a channel for everything, you can focus on the conversations and work that matters most to you.

Channels provide you and your team with a shared view into the work being done. With access to the same information, everyone in the channel can work in sync, and new members have full context when they join.
